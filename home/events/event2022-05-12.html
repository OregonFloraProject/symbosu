What Grows Here? Exploring Oregon's Plant Diversity
More than 4,700 different plant species grow in Oregon, and OregonFlora, recipient of the Museum of Natural and Cultural History's 2021 Oregon Stewardship Award, provides information about all of them. Join botanist and OregonFlora director Linda Hardison to discover the why and where of plant diversity in our state. Registration opens for museum members Tuesday, May 3 and to the public on Wednesday, May 4.
Museum of Natural and Cultural History, University of Oregon
